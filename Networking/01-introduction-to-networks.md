# 01 Introduction to Networks

## What I Have Learned

- What is a network? A network is a group of devices interconnected, which can send and receive messages between them.

- What is a LAN? Local Area Network. It is the connection between devices in a local environment. For example, all of the devices connected in a office form a LAN.

- What is a WAN? Wide Area Network. It is the responsible for making that connection posible over larger distances. For example, If two offices in different cities need to communicate. The connectivity in each office is a LAN, but if you want to connect this two offices you have to use a link called WAN.

- Our modern world is built upon networking. Every transaction, every message, every video, all is send and receive through the links between this devices. Therefore the importance to learn and secure our networks because a great part of our information travels through these links.
